Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to support requests? +
We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 hours during business hours (24/7 coverage). Critical issues are addressed immediately, often within 15 minutes. Enterprise customers receive priority support with guaranteed response times based on their SLA.
What information should I include in my support request? +
Please include: detailed description of the issue, steps to reproduce, expected vs actual behavior, any error messages, screenshots if applicable, which bot is affected, your server ID, and when the issue started. The more details you provide, the faster we can resolve your issue.
Do you offer support in languages other than English? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive support in English and Japanese. Our bilingual team can assist you in your preferred language. We also provide basic support in Spanish, French, and German through our multilingual support specialists.
Can I schedule a consultation for complex integrations? +
Absolutely! We offer video consultations and screen-sharing sessions for complex technical discussions, training, and guided integrations. These sessions can be scheduled through our support portal or by contacting our team directly.
What's included in enterprise support? +
Enterprise support includes: dedicated account manager, priority support queue, custom SLA agreements, phone support, video consultations, integration assistance, training sessions, and 24/7 emergency support. Contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.
How do I report a security vulnerability? +
Security issues should be reported immediately to security@kojinstudios.com. Please do not post security vulnerabilities in public forums or general support channels. We follow responsible disclosure practices and will acknowledge security reports within 24 hours.

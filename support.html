Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get help in your Discord server? +
Join our Discord server and head to the #support channel. Our team monitors it 24/7 and you can also get help from other community members. For urgent issues, mention @Support Team to get immediate attention.
What happens when I submit a support ticket? +
Your ticket is automatically posted to our private support channel in Discord where our team can track, discuss, and resolve your issue. You'll receive updates via email and can also check progress in our Discord server.
Do you offer support in languages other than English? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive support in English and Japanese through our Discord server. We also have community translators who can help with Spanish, French, and German in our international channels.
Can I schedule a one-on-one support session? +
Absolutely! Join our Discord server and use the /schedule command or contact our team directly. We offer screen sharing sessions for complex technical discussions, training, and guided integrations.
What's included in enterprise Discord support? +
Enterprise customers get private Discord channels, dedicated support staff, priority response times, custom SLA agreements, and direct access to our development team for feature requests and integrations.
How do I report a security vulnerability? +
Please DM our Security Team directly in Discord or email security@kojinstudios.com. Do not post security vulnerabilities in public channels. We follow responsible disclosure practices and will respond within 24 hours.
